# Feature Spec: Restore (Healing & Wellness)

> A space with tools for moms to breathe, heal, and come back to themselves.

## Overview

Restore is a wellness and spiritual support library built by moms, for moms. Unlike generic meditation apps, Restore speaks directly to the mom experience — postpartum struggles, identity loss, overwhelm, boundary-setting, recognizing abuse, and the daily grind of doing it all. The client plans to create much of this content herself, making it personal, authentic, and deeply resonant.

## What Makes Restore Different

| Generic Wellness App | Restore |
|---------------------|---------|
| "Breathe in... breathe out..." | "It's 2am and the baby won't sleep and you feel like you're failing. You're not." |
| Secular only | Prayers, affirmations, reiki, meditation — spiritual diversity |
| Created by wellness brands | Created by real moms, including the founder |
| No community | Discussion, sharing what helped, group support |
| $70/year subscription | Free core content, affordable premium |
| Stand-alone app | Connected to a community that knows you |

## User Stories

### As a mom in a moment of overwhelm
- I want to **open the app and immediately find something calming** (not navigate menus)
- I want to **listen to a 2-minute affirmation** that speaks to what I'm feeling right now
- I want to **feel seen** — this content should sound like a friend, not a therapist
- I want to **come back to the same recording** that helped me last time (favorites)

### As a mom seeking ongoing growth
- I want to **work through a guided series** (e.g., 7 days of boundary-setting)
- I want to **learn to recognize unhealthy patterns** in my relationships
- I want to **build a daily practice** (morning affirmation, evening gratitude)
- I want to **choose my spiritual path** (Christian prayers, general spirituality, secular mindfulness, reiki, etc.)

### As a mom who wants to help others
- I want to **share what helped me** with other moms
- I want to **create and contribute content** (if qualified/approved)
- I want to **discuss healing journeys** in a safe group setting

## Core Features

### F1: Content Library
The heart of Restore — a curated, searchable library of healing content.

**Content Types:**
- **Affirmations** — short (1-3 min) spoken affirmations for specific moments
- **Guided meditations** — 5-20 min meditations for various needs
- **Prayers** — from various traditions (Christian, non-denominational, interfaith)
- **Reiki / Energy healing** — guided sessions
- **Breathing exercises** — visual + audio guided
- **Audio guides** — longer-form spoken content on topics like:
  - Recognizing emotional abuse
  - Setting boundaries with family
  - Letting go of mom guilt
  - Finding yourself after becoming a mom
  - Healing from trauma
  - Co-parenting with a difficult ex
- **Written guides** — articles and workbooks on the same topics
- **Video content** — for practices that benefit from visual instruction (yoga, reiki)

**Organization:**
- **By moment:** "I need this right now" — quick access to content for acute feelings
  - I'm overwhelmed
  - I can't sleep
  - I feel like a bad mom
  - I'm angry
  - I'm sad
  - I'm anxious
  - I feel alone
- **By topic:** Browse by subject area
  - Boundaries
  - Self-worth
  - Abuse recovery
  - Postpartum
  - Identity & purpose
  - Relationships
  - Grief & loss
  - Spirituality
  - Body image
  - Mom guilt
- **By practice:** Browse by type of practice
  - Meditation
  - Prayer
  - Affirmations
  - Breathwork
  - Reiki
  - Journaling prompts
  - Yoga & movement
- **By creator:** Browse by who made the content (founder, guest creators)

### F2: "Right Now" Quick Access
- **Emergency calm** — the app opens to a simple screen: "What do you need right now?"
- **Mood selection** — tap how you're feeling, get immediate content recommendations
- **One-tap play** — no menus, no scrolling. Feeling → Content → Play.
- **Recent favorites** — your most-played content is always one tap away
- **Daily suggestion** — based on time of day and past preferences

### F3: Daily Practice Builder
- **Choose your rhythm:** Morning, midday, evening, or bedtime
- **Choose your practice:** Affirmation, meditation, prayer, breathing, journaling
- **Choose your duration:** 2 min, 5 min, 10 min, 20 min
- **Streak tracking** — gentle (not gamified guilt-tripping). "You've shown up for yourself 7 days in a row."
- **Reminders** — optional, customizable notifications
- **Seasonal content** — content that shifts with life stages and seasons

### F4: Guided Series
Multi-day programs on specific topics:
- **"7 Days of Boundaries"** — learn to say no without guilt
- **"Healing After Abuse"** — gentle, paced recovery content (connects to Safe Space)
- **"New Mom, New You"** — postpartum identity and adjustment
- **"Finding Your Voice"** — for moms who've lost themselves in caregiving
- **"Forgiveness Journey"** — 14-day guided process
- **"Spiritual Foundations"** — explore different spiritual practices

Each series includes:
- Daily audio/video content (5-15 min)
- Journaling prompts
- Community discussion (optional)
- Completion recognition

### F5: Community Healing
- **Sharing circles** — moderated group discussions on healing topics
- **"What helped me"** — moms share tools, practices, and turning points
- **Book club** — monthly healing/growth book discussion
- **Prayer/intention groups** — moms supporting each other spiritually
- **Boundaries:** Community features are moderated. This is not therapy and not a crisis service. Clear guidelines.

### F6: Journaling
- **Prompted journaling** — daily prompts tied to content themes
- **Gratitude journal** — quick daily gratitude entry
- **Free writing** — open journal space
- **Private** — journal entries are never shared or visible to anyone
- **Encrypted** — same security standards as Safe Space

### F7: Founder's Corner
The client's personal content — a key differentiator:
- **Audio recordings** created by the founder
- **Personal stories** and reflections
- **Live sessions** (monthly or weekly, depending on capacity)
- **Q&A** — moms can submit questions for the founder to address
- **This is the brand** — the founder's voice and story are what make Restore different from Calm

## Connection to Other Sites

- **→ Safe Space:** "Recognizing abuse" content links directly to Safe Space resources. Gentle, never forced.
- **→ Village:** "Feeling isolated? Find your local mom community." / "Join a sharing circle."
- **→ Launch:** "Ready to turn your passion into purpose? Explore Launch."
- **← All sites:** Restore is the emotional backbone. Any mom on any site can be gently reminded that Restore exists when things get heavy.

## Sensitive Content Guidelines

- **Not therapy.** Clear disclaimers that Restore is wellness content, not professional mental health treatment.
- **Crisis redirects.** If journaling or community content suggests crisis (self-harm keywords, acute danger), provide immediate resources:
  - 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
  - Crisis Text Line
  - Safe Space (for DV)
- **Trauma-informed.** All content should be trauma-informed — no surprise triggers, content warnings where appropriate, user controls pace.
- **No toxic positivity.** "Good vibes only" is not the brand. Honest, real, sometimes messy — that's the brand.
- **Diverse voices.** Content should represent diverse racial, cultural, and socioeconomic backgrounds.

- Content library with 20-30 pieces (affirmations, meditations, guides)
- "Right Now" quick access (mood → content)
- Basic favorites and history
- Founder's Corner (initial audio content)
- Simple daily reminder

### Phase 2
- Daily practice builder with streaks
- Guided series (3-5 multi-day programs)
- Journaling (prompted and free)
- Guest creator content
- Prayer/spiritual practice diversity expansion

### Phase 3
- Community healing features (sharing circles, discussions)
- Live sessions with the founder
- Content marketplace (guest creators sell premium content)
- Personalized recommendations based on usage patterns
- Integration with wearables (calming exercises triggered by heart rate)

## Revenue Model
- **Free:** Core content library (20-30 pieces), "Right Now" quick access, daily reminders
- **Premium ($6.99/month):** Full library, guided series, daily practice builder, journaling, no ads
- **Content marketplace:** Guest creators sell premium series (platform takes 30%)
- **Bundled with MomConnect Premium:** Discount when subscribed to multiple sites

## Key Metrics
- Daily active listeners
- Content completion rate (do people finish the meditation or drop off?)
- Return rate (% who come back within 24 hours)
- Favorites saved
- Streak length (daily practice)
- Series completion rate
- Net Promoter Score (do moms recommend Restore?)
- Crisis redirect usage (important safety metric)

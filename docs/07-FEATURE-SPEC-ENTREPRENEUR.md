# Feature Spec: Launch (Entrepreneur Hub)

> Where moms build businesses, find mentors, and turn ideas into income.

## Overview

Launch is a business development platform designed around the reality of being a mom entrepreneur: interrupted schedules, limited startup capital, and the need for flexible, supportive mentorship. It combines AI business tools with human mentor matching and a direct pipeline to Market for selling.

## User Stories

### As a mom with a business idea
- I want to **explore my idea with an AI partner** that helps me think through viability, market, and first steps
- I want to **find a mentor** who has done something similar
- I want to **take a short course** on business basics (not a 40-hour MBA)
- I want to **create a simple business plan** without jargon
- I want to **connect with other moms** at the same stage

### As a mom with an existing business
- I want to **find collaborators** (a designer, an accountant, a social media manager)
- I want to **mentor newer entrepreneurs** and give back
- I want to **list my services on Market** directly from my Launch profile
- I want to **access business tools** (invoice templates, pricing calculators, social media schedulers)
- I want to **find grants and funding** I'm eligible for

### As a mom re-entering the workforce
- I want to **explore what I could do** with my skills
- I want to **build confidence** through small wins
- I want to **find flexible work** posted by other mom entrepreneurs

## Core Features

### F1: AI Business Ideation Partner
- **Conversational interface** — "I love baking and everyone says I should sell my cookies. Is that realistic?"
- **Guided exploration** — AI walks through:
  - What you're good at / what you love
  - Who would pay for it
  - What it would take to start (time, money, skills)
  - First 3 concrete steps
- **Local market analysis** — "There are 12 mom bakeries in your area. Here's what they charge and what niches are open."
- **Business model suggestions** — subscription boxes, local delivery, farmers market, online shop
- **Not a replacement for real advice** — always suggests talking to a mentor or SCORE advisor too

### F2: Micro-Courses
- **5-15 minute lessons** designed for interrupted schedules
- **Core tracks:**
  - Starting Your Business (idea → first sale)
  - Pricing & Money (how to price, basic bookkeeping, taxes)
  - Marketing on Zero Budget (social media, word of mouth, local marketing)
  - Legal Basics (LLC, permits, insurance — state-specific where possible)
  - Selling Online (setting up your Market shop, photography, descriptions)
  - Scaling Up (hiring help, systems, growing beyond yourself)
- **Created by:** Real mom entrepreneurs (not generic business content)
- **Format:** Video + text + worksheet for each lesson
- **Progress tracking:** Resume where you left off, earn completion badges
- **Discussion:** Each lesson has a comments section for Q&A

### F3: Mentor Matching
- **Mentor profiles** — experienced mom entrepreneurs volunteer their time
- **Matching criteria:**
  - Industry/skill alignment
  - Location (for local mentors)
  - Availability and schedule compatibility
  - Stage of business (early idea vs. scaling)
- **Structure:** Monthly 30-minute video calls (not open-ended, prevents burnout)
- **Mentor recognition:** Badges, featured profiles, "give back" incentives
- **Group mentorship** — one mentor, 3-5 mentees at similar stages (more efficient, peer support)

### F4: Collaboration Board
- **Post what you need:** "Looking for a graphic designer to create my logo"
- **Post what you offer:** "I'm a CPA and can help with quarterly taxes"
- **Categories:** Design, Marketing, Legal, Accounting, Technology, Photography, Childcare, Other
- **Skill exchange:** Trade services instead of paying (I'll design your logo if you photograph my products)
- **Trust scores** from the shared MomConnect platform

### F5: Resource Library
- **Grant finder** — searchable database of grants for women entrepreneurs, mom-owned businesses, minority-owned businesses
- **Template library** — business plan templates, invoice templates, contract templates, social media templates
- **Tool recommendations** — curated list of free/affordable tools for accounting, design, website building, social media
- **Local resources** — SBA offices, SCORE chapters, women's business centers near you
- **Legal checklist** — state-specific requirements for starting a business

### F6: Job & Gig Board
- **Flexible work** posted by mom entrepreneurs for other moms
- **Categories:** Part-time, freelance, project-based, remote
- **Mom-friendly filters:** School hours only, work from home, bring your kids
- **Direct from Launch businesses** — entrepreneurs who've grown through Launch can hire through Launch

## Connection to Other Sites

- **→ Market:** "Your business plan is ready. Create your first listing on Market."
- **→ Village:** "Find local networking events and business meetups."
- **→ Restore:** "Entrepreneur burnout is real. Here are resources for you."
- **← Safe Space:** Women leaving DV situations can find financial independence through Launch.

## MVP vs. Full Version

### MVP
- AI business ideation partner (conversational)
- 3-5 initial micro-courses (Starting Your Business, Pricing, Marketing)
- Basic collaboration board (post needs/offers)
- Resource library (grants, templates, local SBA/SCORE links)
- Direct link to create a Market listing

### Phase 2
- Mentor matching
- Full course library (10+ tracks)
- Job & gig board
- Progress tracking and badges
- Group mentorship

### Phase 3
- AI-powered grant matching
- Advanced business tools (invoicing, bookkeeping integrations)
- Mastermind groups (structured peer accountability)
- Annual virtual conference
- Investor connections (for businesses ready to scale)

## Revenue Model
- **Free tier:** AI ideation, basic courses, collaboration board, resource library
- **Premium ($9.99/month):** All courses, mentor matching, advanced tools, grant alerts
- **Course marketplace:** Mom entrepreneurs can sell their own courses (platform takes 20%)

## Key Metrics
- Businesses started (tracked through Market listings or self-reported)
- Course completions
- Mentor matches made
- Collaboration board connections
- Revenue generated by Launch members on Market
- Time from "I have an idea" to "I made my first sale"

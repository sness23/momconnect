# MomConnect Platform — Open Questions

Questions we need to discuss with the client before going deeper on feature specs.

## Business & Vision

- [ ] **Platform name?** MomConnect is a working title. What does the client want to call it?
- [ ] **Who is the team?** Is the client the sole founder? Does she have a co-founder, designer, or other collaborators?
- [ ] **Budget range?** This affects whether we build custom or use existing platforms/templates. Even a rough range helps (e.g., under $5k, $5-20k, $20-50k, $50k+).
- [ ] **Revenue model thoughts?** We've suggested transaction fees + premium content. Does the client have opinions on pricing?
- [ ] **Is the client the brand?** Will she be the face of the platform? This affects branding, content strategy, and the healing content approach.
- [ ] **Timeline expectations?** Is there a launch date in mind, or is this "when it's ready"?

## Safe Space (Most Sensitive)

- [ ] **Legal review?** DV resources carry liability. Has the client consulted with a lawyer or DV organization?
- [ ] **Partnerships?** Does the client have relationships with DV organizations, hotlines, or shelters? These partnerships are critical for credibility and safety.
- [ ] **AI boundaries?** The AI safety planner is powerful but risky. What should it NOT do? (e.g., it should never advise a woman to confront her abuser)
- [ ] **Moderation?** Who moderates content and reports? This needs human oversight, not just AI.
- [ ] **Anonymity model?** Can someone use Safe Space without any connection to their MomConnect identity?

## Content & Community

- [ ] **Healing content:** The client mentioned making content herself. What format? (audio recordings, written guides, video?) How much exists already?
- [ ] **Entrepreneur courses:** Will these be created by the client, by community members, or curated from existing resources?
- [ ] **Moderation team?** Community platforms need moderators. Who does this? Volunteers? Paid staff? AI-assisted?
- [ ] **Geographic scope?** Starting in one city/region, or nationwide from day one? US only, or international?

## Technical

- [ ] **Existing domain?** Does the client own any domains?
- [ ] **Existing brand assets?** Logo, colors, fonts, mood boards?
- [ ] **Mobile vs web?** The conversation mentions "app" — is mobile-first important, or is web-first OK with mobile later?
- [ ] **Hosting budget?** Monthly budget for servers, AI APIs, etc.?

## Priority

- [ ] **Which site first?** If we build one at a time, which one is the client most passionate about launching first?
- [ ] **MVP scope?** For the first site, what is the absolute minimum that would be exciting and useful?

# MomConnect — Client Action Steps & Open Questions

Things we need from you before (and while) we build! None of this has to be perfect — rough answers, voice memos, Pinterest boards, whatever works. We just need enough to keep moving.

---

## Action Steps (Do These Now!)

These are things you can start on right away while we build the foundation and Village.

### 1. Choose a Name
MomConnect is a working title. Do you love it, or do you have something else in mind? This affects the domain, branding, and everything visual — so the sooner we know, the better. (It's also totally fine to stick with MomConnect!)

### 2. Brand Direction
We don't need a full brand guide yet — just a vibe. Any of these would help:
- A Pinterest board of colors, fonts, or designs you like
- Screenshots of apps or websites that feel right
- Words that describe the feeling (e.g., "warm but modern", "earthy and real", "bright and playful")
- Colors you love or hate
- Any existing logo ideas, even a napkin sketch

### 3. Start Recording Restore Content
You mentioned creating healing content yourself — this is a huge differentiator and we'll need it for Phase 2. Start now, even if it's rough:
- **10 affirmations** (1-3 min each, various themes — motherhood, self-worth, healing, strength)
- **5 guided meditations** (5-10 min each)
- **5 audio guides** (10-20 min on topics you're passionate about — boundaries, mom guilt, trauma healing, etc.)
- **3 prayers or spiritual pieces**

Phone recordings are totally fine for now. We can re-record with better audio later. The important thing is getting your voice and message captured.

### 4. Invite Your First 20 Moms
Village (the community space) is launching first, and a community needs people! Start thinking about:
- 20 moms you know personally who would try it out
- Local moms' groups you're part of
- Anyone excited about what you're building

These early moms will shape the whole platform. Their feedback is gold.

### 5. Research DV Organizations
Safe Space is the most sensitive part of the platform and we need professional partners before it goes live. Start having conversations with:
- Local domestic violence shelters in your area
- The [National DV Hotline](https://www.thehotline.org/) — they may have partnership programs
- Any DV advocates or social workers in your network
- Legal aid organizations that work with DV survivors

Even a single partnership makes Safe Space credible and safe. This takes time, so starting early is key.

### 6. Answer the Open Questions Below
Read through and give us whatever answers you have. "I don't know yet" is a valid answer — we just want to know where your head is at.

## Open Questions

### Business & Vision

- [ ] **Platform name?** MomConnect is a working title. What do you want to call it?
- [ ] **Who is the team?** Are you the sole founder? Do you have a co-founder, designer, or other collaborators?
- [ ] **Budget range?** This affects build vs. buy decisions. Even a rough range helps (under $5k, $5-20k, $20-50k, $50k+).
- [ ] **Revenue model thoughts?** We've suggested transaction fees (Market) + premium subscriptions (Restore, Launch). Does that feel right? Opinions on pricing?
- [ ] **Are you the brand?** Will you be the face of the platform? This affects branding, the Founder's Corner in Restore, and content strategy.
- [ ] **Timeline expectations?** Is there a target launch date, or is this "when it's ready"?

### Safe Space

- [ ] **Legal review?** DV resources carry liability. Have you consulted with a lawyer or DV organization?
- [ ] **Partnerships?** Do you have existing relationships with DV organizations, hotlines, or shelters?
- [ ] **AI boundaries?** The AI safety planner is powerful but needs guardrails. What should it absolutely NOT do?
- [ ] **Moderation?** Who handles reports and moderates sensitive content? This needs human oversight.
- [ ] **Anonymity model?** Can someone use Safe Space with zero connection to their MomConnect identity?

### Content & Community

- [ ] **Healing content format?** You mentioned making content yourself — audio? Written guides? Video? How much exists already?
- [ ] **Entrepreneur courses?** Will you create these, curate them from existing resources, or invite community members to contribute?
- [ ] **Moderation team?** Community platforms need moderators. Volunteers? Paid staff? AI-assisted?
- [ ] **Geographic scope?** Starting in one city/region, or nationwide from day one? US only?

### Technical

- [ ] **Existing domain?** Do you own any domains you want to use? (We're starting on sness.net subdomains either way.)
- [ ] **Existing brand assets?** Logo, colors, fonts, mood boards — anything at all?
- [ ] **Mobile vs. web?** Is mobile-first important, or is web-first OK with a mobile app later?
- [ ] **Hosting budget?** Rough monthly budget for servers and API costs?

### Priority

- [ ] **Which site first?** We're planning Village first — does that feel right to you, or is there another space you're more passionate about launching?
- [ ] **MVP scope?** For Village, what's the absolute minimum that would be exciting and useful to your first 20 moms?
